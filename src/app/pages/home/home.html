About Me

Where material
meets meaning

I'm Federica Moro — an artist working with clay and fiber to explore structure, softness, and the poetry of everyday forms.

My ceramics are hand-built and thrown on the wheel, often textured and glazed to reflect organic surfaces — soil, weathered walls, skin. My textile work weaves together natural fibers, color, and pattern as a way of mapping memory and place.

Based in Italy, open to collaborations, commissions, and collectors worldwide.

Inspiration

What moves me

◈

Nature & Materials

Clay, linen, wool, stone — the textures of the natural world translated into form through hands and time.

◎

Memory & Place

The textures of childhood landscapes, Italian light, and the quiet persistence of handmade things passed down through generations.

◆

The Tactile & the Slow

Resisting speed. Making by hand as a practice of attention — where the time spent in making becomes part of the work itself.
